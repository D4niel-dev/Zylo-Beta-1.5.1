Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Click the + button in the sidebar to create or join a group.

How does the XP system work?

You gain XP by sending messages. Leveling up unlocks profile effects!

Is Zylo secure?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy. Check Settings > Security for more.

Can I customize my theme?

Absolutely. Go to Settings > Appearance to choose or create themes.

How do I add friends?

Go to Friends tab and click "Add Friend". Enter their username to send a request.

Can I send files in DMs?

Yes! Click the + icon in the chat input to upload images, videos, or documents.

What are stickers?

Stickers are fun, larger images you can send in chat. Click the sticker icon to browse!

How do I record voice messages?

Hold the microphone button in any chat input to record and send a voice clip.

How do I search for messages?

Use the search bar in the top right to find specific keywords or messages across channels.

Can I block users?

Yes. Click on a user's profile and select "Block" to prevent them from messaging you.

What is "Moments"?

Moments is a feed where you can share photos and updates with your friends, similar to a social feed.

Is there a limit to My Cloud storage?

Currently, Zylo offers 500MB of cloud storage for all users to save their files and media.

How do I change my status?

Zylo automatically detects if you are away. You can also manually set your status in settings.

Can I mute specific groups or friends?

Yes! Click the bell icon in the chat header to toggle notifications for that specific chat.

Is AI Chat private?

Your AI Chat history is stored locally and is not shared with other users or publicly.

How can I suggest new features?

We love feedback! You can reach out to the developer directly through the About Us section.
