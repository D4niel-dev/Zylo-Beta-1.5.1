## Relationships
- **Aris (Father):** He entrusted her with the motherly role, perhaps implicitly or explicitly. They share a deep bond of mutual reliance for the family's well-being.
- **Dizel & Zylia & Filt:** She cares for them all, likely fussing over them and ensuring they are fed and safe.
- **Filt:** Shares a similar calmness, but their expressions of anger are different facets of quiet intensity.
